MASTANG

Features
ENGINE

Its 5.8-liter supercharged V-8 engine generates 662 horsepower and 631 lb-ft of torque. It can accelerate from zero to 60 mph in just 3.5 seconds, and its braking distance from 60 mph to zero is only 101 feet.

SIZE

Dimensions(in mm) Length 4784 , Width 1961 , Height 1391 , Wheelbase 2720.

HISTORY

The Ford Mustang was first introduced at the 1964 New York World's Fair. Its launch price was $2,368, making it accessible to many drivers. The vehicle was introduced four months before the start of the 1965 production year and manufactured alongside the 1964 Mercury Comet and Ford Falcon.
